
Some Notes on the van't Veer gene set ~ Christine Staiger ~ Nov 2012
=======================================================================

Gene Signature published in:

Gene expression profiling predicts clinical outcome of breast cancer
van 't Veer LJ, Dai H, van de Vijver MJ, He YD, Hart AA, Mao M, Peterse HL, 
van der Kooy K, Marton MJ, Witteveen AT, Schreiber GJ, Kerkhoven RM, Roberts C, Linsley PS, Bernards R, Friend SH 
Nature

Of the 70 published probes 55 mapped to genes from which 41 could be mapped to
Entrez geneID. The geneset is also contained in the MsigDB C2 set under the name VANTVEER_BREAST_CANCER_POOR_PROGNOSIS


Some Notes on the Erasmus gene set ~ Christine Staiger ~ Nov 2012
=======================================================================
Gene Signature published in:

Gene-expression profiles to predict distant metastasis of lymph-node-negative primary breast cancer.
Wang Y, Klijn JG, Zhang Y, Sieuwerts AM, Look MP, Yang F, Talantov D, Timmermans M, Meijer-van Gelder ME, 
Yu J, Jatkoe T, Berns EM, Atkins D, Foekens JA.
Lancet. 2005 Feb 19-25;365(9460):671-9.

The 76 published probes map to 66 unique geneIDs. All of the probes are present on the affy U133A array.
There are two versions of the feature extractor. One is defined on the affy probes and one on the mapped 
geneIDs.

Original Gene signature: ErasmusMC_Wang_genes.csv, getErasmusGenes.py
